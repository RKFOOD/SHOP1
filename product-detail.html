Product Description

Our Organic Onion Powder is made from the finest organic onions, carefully selected and processed to preserve their natural flavor and nutritional benefits. This versatile seasoning adds a rich, savory depth to any dish without the need for chopping fresh onions.

Perfect for soups, stews, marinades, rubs, and dressings, our onion powder is a must-have in every kitchen. The powder dissolves easily, ensuring even distribution of flavor throughout your dishes.

  • 100% Organic and Non-GMO
  • No additives or preservatives
  • Rich in antioxidants and vitamins
  • Gluten-free and vegan
  • Long shelf life

Ingredients

100% Organic Dehydrated Onion

Allergen Information: Packed in a facility that also processes tree nuts, peanuts, and sesame seeds.

How to Use

Add 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder per serving to enhance the flavor of your dishes. Here are some ideas:

  • Sprinkle on roasted vegetables before cooking
  • Mix into burger patties or meatballs
  • Add to soups, stews, and sauces
  • Use in dry rubs for meats
  • Incorporate into homemade spice blends

Storage: Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. For best flavor, use within 6 months of opening.
