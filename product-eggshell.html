Product Description

Our Organic Eggshell Powder is made from the finest organic eggshells, carefully cleaned, sterilized, and ground into a fine powder. This natural source of calcium is easily absorbed by the body and can be added to various foods and beverages.

Each batch is tested for purity and quality to ensure you receive only the best product. Our eggshell powder is an excellent way to reduce food waste while providing essential nutrients to support your health.

Health Benefits

  • Rich in bioavailable calcium for strong bones and teeth
  • Contains collagen and other beneficial proteins
  • May help support joint health
  • Natural source of minerals like magnesium and phosphorus
  • May help with acid-alkaline balance in the body

How to Use

Add 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon of Organic Eggshell Powder to your daily routine. It can be mixed with:

  • Smoothies and shakes
  • Yogurt or oatmeal
  • Baked goods
  • Soups and sauces
  • Homemade pet food (for pets)

For best results, store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. One teaspoon of powder contains approximately 800-1,000 mg of calcium.

Note: If you have any medical conditions or are taking medications, please consult with your healthcare provider before using this product as a calcium supplement.
